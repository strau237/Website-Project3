ABOUT THE GAME

Dungeons and Dragons is a fantasy role-playing game that allows players to come together to create their own characters and world to engage in a immersive fantasy adventure. Led by a dungeon master, players will go on quests, gather power-ups and treasure, and engage in strategic team puzzle solving. For more information about game basics and background, please visit the 'Resource' tab located above.

Our group meets every Monday from 7pm to 10pm (scheduling of the guild permitting) to play three hour adventure sessions, with occasional meetings set aside for character, world, and team-building activities and planning sessions. During the fall semester, all meetings will be held at 1202 7th Street SE in Dinkytown, the home of this guild's current Dungeon Master Derek Frank. All players are asked to bring their log and character sheets, BYOB and snacks, and any ideas for the night's session!

To stay updated with session outcomes, please visit the 'Session Logs' page above, and make sure to check out the 'Announcements' page to stay up to date with meeting times and game updates.
